my Work

This was an illustration I did for an app called Replica, an app for screen mirroring. You can check it out on their website by clicking in here.

This next work aimed to mimic the representation of an animal (ocean fish), and then to perform different studies, capturing the global morphology of the animal through the process of simplification by flattening and by accentuation, while exploring the line.

 

The letter has a semantic potential of its own if we consider only its spatial distribution and the evocative character of its forms. This work aimed to explore the potential of communication and expression of the letters and to carry out and deepen the studies using words and drawings of chosen letters as a starting point.

 

The cross between graphic design and cinema. 2022 marked the 100th anniversary of the film "Nosferatu, the Vampire" by F. W. Murnau. This anniversary is the perfect occasion to develop a creative project around cinema, like this logo I designed.
